This 18 × 29” syllabus explores the purpose of notation,
the format of knowledge, feedback loops, and a tool to
un/re/learn historical narratives. By actively integrating
the perspectives of diverse academics and artists,

Western-centric world, the syllabus fosters a nuanced
amalgamation of viewpoints.
